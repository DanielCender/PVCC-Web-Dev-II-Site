Operating Systems.

A user's OS may not seem to impact the web developer much, but that first impression can be decieving. As the iPhone has aged, more and more of the world has opted to go without a laptop/desktop screen entirely, sticking to just their mobile devices.

A developer of the modern era must be keen on devising layouts that are well-suited for the mobile user looking at a phone or tablet screen. The data tables below are measures of OS traffic to W3Schools' site in the designated time frame. Now, this data suggests a small quantity of mobile users at large, but that is quite tricky to say for sure. As W3Schools often serves as a reference tool for developers and students, many consumer "netizens" will never come anywhere close to this site.

To that extent, this data might be better interpreted as - The Ranking of OS's Used in Development by professionals, students, and hobbyists.
