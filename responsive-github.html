GitHub.

In case any people weren't paying attention, GitHub has exploded in the past few years as a forerunner in managing code workflows for teams, both organized and open-source. It contains such a mass of codebases for different industries that it can be comprehensively used as a measure of the direction development is trending towards.

In 2017, there were a grand total of 24 million developers working across roughly 67 million repositories. The most prolific regions for accounts are Asia, North America, and Europe. Click the buttons below to see the region's percentage of the global total.
